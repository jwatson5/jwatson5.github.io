Groups

Forming Groups

  • 4 or 5 people per group
  • Weekly group update on Canvas group discussion
    • Meeting times and members present
    • Tasks assigned that week and to whom
    • Task accomplished that week and by whom
  • In-class activities will contribute but you WILL need to work together outside of class
  • Similar working schedules, but diversity of backgrounds makes the best group!

Envisioning Cards

Envisioning Cards

  • View Envisioning Cards
  • Considering the long-term influence of new technology
    • As it spans across time
    • Becomes pervasive in society
    • Affects the lives of different stakeholders
    • Raises issues that touch human values
  • Goal is to evoke consideration and broadening of ideas during technology design.

Activity

Part 1

  • Choose one of the following systems:
    • eReader and supporting applications
    • xBox Kinect
    • fitBit and software
    • Any other new application/system you are somewhat familiar with
  • Choose ONE card from each of the 4 categories – choose randomly, but if it really doesn’t fit, try another
  • Use timer to discuss each card.
  • Write up neatly, on paper or digitally
    • Write names of everyone present
  • One person present one of the results to the class

Part 2

  • Brainstorm 5 potential project topics
    • Help an expert with some aspect of their job/activity
  • Write one positive and one negative aspect of choosing each as your project topic
  • Choose one!

  • Turn in your document to Canvas

  • Also, time permitting:
    • come up with a group name
    • set a default meeting time/place
